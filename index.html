Welcome to SmartUrbanity

SmartUrbanity is a research and innovation project co-funded by the European Union's DUT Partnership. It pioneers digital and participatory tools to support the transition toward 15-minute cities across Europe.

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize urban planning by making cities more accessible, inclusive, and sustainable through the use of smart data, citizen engagement platforms, and policy support tools.

Key Features

  • Citizen Engagement App for community-based data collection
  • WebGIS-based Accessibility Analysis Platform
  • Decision Support System (DSS) for informed policymaking
  • Gamification strategies to enhance participation

Where We're Working

Pilot implementations are underway in:

  • Rome (Italy)
  • Karlsruhe (Germany)
  • İzmir (Türkiye)
  • Lyon Metropolitan Area (France)
  • Greater Zurich Area (Switzerland)
