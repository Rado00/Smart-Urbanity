Data & Digital Tools

SmartUrbanity integrates advanced tools and participatory platforms to support decision-makers, researchers, and citizens in building 15-minute cities.

1. Citizen Engagement App

A mobile application for collecting behavioral, mobility, and perception data from residents. It enables interactive surveys, crowd-sourced feedback, and gamified participation incentives.

2. Accessibility Analysis Platform (WebGIS)

A geospatial analysis tool that visualizes accessibility gaps at the neighborhood scale. It helps local planners assess how easily citizens can reach essential services by foot, bike, or public transport.

3. Decision Support System (DSS)

A multi-criteria platform designed to guide urban planning and policy decisions. It integrates user feedback, urban data, and accessibility scores to support informed interventions.

4. Gamification Framework

Game-like features embedded in the app and platform to increase civic engagement, especially among youth and underrepresented groups. Includes rewards, challenges, and interactive maps.
